Our Story

A family table on Ballard Avenue

San Fermo started with a simple idea: cook honest food from great ingredients and share it with the neighborhood. Since 2016, we've been doing exactly that from our spot on Ballard Avenue.

Our kitchen draws on the regional traditions of northern Italy, but the ingredients come from close to home. We work directly with Pacific Northwest farms, ranches, and fisheries, many of which we've partnered with since day one. The menu changes with the seasons because our sourcing does too.

We farm 34 acres of our own at Swan House Farm in the Skagit Valley, where we raise animals, tend orchards, and grow produce that finds its way to your plate. The connection between the farm and the restaurant is at the heart of everything we do.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

We've replaced traditional tipping with a 23% service charge on dine-in orders and 10% on to-go. This supports fair wages across the entire team. Unlike tips, service charge is subject to Washington State sales tax.
We have three indoor dining rooms and several heated tables on our deck. Requests for specific areas can be made but are not guaranteed. Reservations book the table for two hours. Our last seating is 9 PM weeknights and 9:30 PM Friday and Saturday.
We are very family friendly. Please indicate if you need a high chair and be sure to include all members of your party when booking so we can plan the right table.
We have two tables on our front porch and during summer four additional sidewalk cafe tables that can accommodate pets. These tables cannot be reserved, so there may be a wait if you plan to bring your pooch.
Our to-go menu goes live nightly at 4 PM. Follow the Order To-Go link to place your order through ChowNow.
All food and beverage must be ordered from our menu and wine list. We don't have a corkage policy or a cake fee.
